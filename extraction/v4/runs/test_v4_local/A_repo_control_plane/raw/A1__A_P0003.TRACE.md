   - references[]:
     - ref_type ("service"|"command"|"file_path"|"env_var"|"mcp_server"|"model"|"other")
     - ref_value (literal string)
     - source_path
     - anchor_excerpt

- REPO_INSTRUCTION_SURFACE.json
